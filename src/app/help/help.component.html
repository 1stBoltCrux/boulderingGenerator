What is a bouldering problem?

A bouldering problem is essentially a short climb with a designated set of starting, intermediary and finishing holds. They are rated (in the yosemite system) from V-B to about V-16, the higher the number, the harder the problem.

Our Bouldering Generator with allow you the freedom of creating your own routes on our bouldering wall with a difficulty between VB and V10.

Using our app:
  1. Enter your Username and select the difficulty of the route you wish to create.
  2. Select the holds you wish to be included in your route. Starting hold(s) will highlight green, finishing hold(s) will highlight red, and all other holds will highlight blue. All routes in the top row are considered finishing routes and will highlight red when clicked.
  3. Click "Submit" to add your shiny new route to the app!
